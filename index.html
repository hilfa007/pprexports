Who We Are

PPR Exporters is a kerala based registered certified export and import company that operates on a global scale. We render our service to customers world wide,offering best quality products at affordable prices and excellent customer service. Our supply categories include fresh fruits and vegitables,spices and groceries. We protect and advance the interests of our customers by monitoring the market trends, exchanging market knowledge, and being proactive in responding to changing circumstances.

Services
01
Fruits

India's diverse climate ensures the availability of all kinds of fresh fruit and vegetables. It ranks as the second-largest producer of fruit and vegetables in the world. Grapes, pomegranates, mangoes, bananas and oranges account for the largest proportion of the country's fruit exports.

02
Vegetables

Vegetables represent a large part of the production in India. Because of the large production, We can export quality vegetables in enormous quantity at an affordable price. Onions, Mixed Vegetables, Potatoes, Tomatoes, and Green Chilly contribute largely to the vegetable export basket.

03
Groceries

Food and groceries are the largest segment of the retail business in India, the third largest food and grocery market in the world.
New product categories like added dairy , RTE products and snacks are driving growth.We supply quality grocery items at reasonable price.

04
Spices

Indian spices are the most sought after on a global scale, due to their exquisite fragrance, texture, taste and medicinal value. India is the home of spices such as Chilly, Turmeric, Cumin, Coriander, Ginger, Fenugreek, Cardamom, curry powder, Oils Oleoresins, Garlic and Mint products.
